Who we are

Students, professionals, specialists

We are creative people and passionate about what we believe

The Rotfather project is developed by the research group G2E - Education and the Entertainment Group from the Design course at the Federal University of Santa Catarina. Under the coordination of Professor Dr. Monica Stein, in our team, we have graduate and undergraduate students, from different areas: design, animation, design management, advertising, cinema, games, information systems, music, and others.
