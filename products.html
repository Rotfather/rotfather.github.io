The Rotfather products

The Rotfather is a transmedia project that has a complex mafia storyworld spread differently in a good number of mediums, beside the 2D Game, it is been developed a 2D animated series, comic books, audio books, card games, boardgames, a short movie, a movie, licensed and merchandising products.

The Animed 2D Series

The 2D animation series consist in four seasons, composed of 13 epidodes. It will possible to discover some special things about the story, as a character insterest and motivations. The story is like a puzzle whre the pieces can be collected and connected during the episodes and seasons.

The Comic Books (HQ)

There are 5 comic books, each one tells the story of one character, the comics allows to know the characters, their relationships and intrigues.

The Audio Books

The stoyline is about a rat journalist. Her investigations about on crimes shows a city - with different facets - and surroundings - full of animals looking for power. Through her eyes, lots of mysteries can be seen - corruption in the political scene and inside the media. In this world, the sugar mafia system is only one more more facet of this city, and target of her investigations too. It is possible to see as well, how the “city” treats the ones who advantures themselves trying to investigate the crimes.

Books

The story is about a rat detective who was Al Kane’s best friend when they were children, and now tries to restore order in the sewers, where cases and more cases of violence and murderer associated to the sugar mafia happen all the time. It is a story of someone who wants to restore the order in the system, but every time he thinks he can do that, he finds his old friend behind a crime, and gets confused.

Card Games and Board Games

Both uses aspects of the sugar mafia system to create mechanics , rules, goals, and experiences where the playes can be a mafia guy or not. In some games, the player have to collaborate with a mafia colleague, in others the player will have to kill the mafia guys.

Movies

It`s a story of surviving in a very complicated system and how to get rif of that.
